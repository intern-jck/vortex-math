Vortex Graph Maker

The colors of the lines represent their length.
Red is the longest and blue is the shortest
with yellows, greens and purples
being somewhere in between.

Download the above graph as an SVG file for lasercutting.
The SVG is just about 100mm in diameter.
The inner lines are colored blue for engraving
and the circle's outline is colored black for cutting.
